ESTELLA Portfolio Website

How to use:
1. Open index.html in your browser.
2. Replace images/me-1.svg to images/me-4.svg with your own photos.
   If you use JPG photos, update the photos array in script.js from .svg to .jpg.
3. The navigation bar is HOME | ESTELLA | RESUME | CONTACT.
4. Resume downloads use resume-en.docx and resume-cn.docx.
5. Language switch is shared across pages and saved in localStorage.
